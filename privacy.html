Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Zero Data Collection Policy

1. Information We Don't Collect

We believe your health data is yours alone. We do not collect, store, or process any personal health information.

When you use our BMI calculator or any other tool on this site:

  • Your height, weight, age, and other inputs are processed entirely in your browser
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • No cookies are used to track your health information
  • No account creation is required

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to optionally save your calculation history on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your browser
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time through your browser settings
  • Is used solely for your convenience (e.g., comparing previous calculations)

3. Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand general site usage patterns. This data is:

  • Fully anonymized
  • Does not include any health information
  • Used only to improve site functionality and content

4. Advertising

We display advertisements to support the free operation of this site. Our advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or by visiting AboutAds.info.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@bmitool.org.
