Our Philosophy

We believe privacy is a right, not a privilege. FastingTool is designed to give you useful information without harvesting your personal data. Most of what you do on this site stays on your device.

What We Collect

Honestly? Almost nothing. When you visit FastingTool, we may collect:

  • Anonymous usage data — page views, time on site, and general traffic patterns. This helps us understand which articles are helpful and which need work.
  • Device information — browser type, screen size, and operating system. We use this to make sure the site works well on your device.

We do not collect your name, email address, weight, fasting schedule, or any health information unless you voluntarily email us.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies to improve your experience. These include basic analytics cookies that help us understand site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our site. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Security

While we collect very little data, what we do collect is stored securely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect against unauthorized access.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@fastingtool.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please email us at contact@fastingtool.org.
