Calculator Limitations

The calculators on this site provide estimates based on population-level data and standard formulas. They have known limitations:

  • BMI does not distinguish between muscle and fat mass
  • Pediatric BMI percentiles are approximations based on CDC growth charts
  • Ethnicity-specific thresholds are guidelines, not absolute rules
  • Individual metabolic health requires clinical examination and laboratory testing
  • Results should not be used for self-diagnosis or self-treatment
