WHO Western Pacific Guidelines

Asian BMI Threshold Calculator

Asian populations have different metabolic risk profiles at lower BMI values. The WHO Western Pacific Region recommends lower cutoffs: overweight at 23, obesity at 25. This calculator applies ethnicity-specific thresholds.

Data stays on your device
Medical Disclaimer: Asian-specific BMI thresholds are based on WHO Western Pacific Region and IDF consensus. Individual risk varies by genetics, diet, and lifestyle. Always consult your healthcare provider.
