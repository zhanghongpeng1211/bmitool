Medical Disclaimer

BMITool.org provides educational information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

The calculators, articles, and resources on this site are for general informational purposes. They should not be used as a replacement for consultation with a qualified healthcare provider, including physicians, registered dietitians, or certified fitness professionals.

Always seek the advice of your healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding your weight, metabolic health, or fitness. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you have read on this website.

BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic of body fatness or health. Muscle mass, bone density, body composition, and fat distribution are not captured by BMI calculations. Individual health assessment requires clinical examination and comprehensive evaluation.

Children and adolescents require age and sex-specific BMI percentile evaluation, not adult BMI categories. Asian populations may require ethnicity-specific BMI thresholds for accurate metabolic risk assessment.
