About BMI Tool

BMI Tool was created in early 2024 after I noticed a frustrating pattern: most BMI calculators online were either cluttered with ads, required app downloads, or gave conflicting results. I wanted something clean, accurate, and genuinely helpful.

Our Mission

We believe understanding your health metrics should be simple and accessible. No accounts. No downloads. No paywalls. Just a reliable tool that works on any device, anywhere in the world.

What Makes Us Different

  • WHO-Standard Formula: We use the exact calculation method recommended by the World Health Organization, not simplified approximations.
  • Dual Unit Support: Switch seamlessly between metric and imperial without losing accuracy.
  • Privacy First: We do not store your height, weight, or results. Everything happens in your browser.
  • Mobile Optimized: Designed for phones first, because that is where most people check their BMI.

Who We Are

I am a health-tech enthusiast based in Southeast Asia. In 2023, I spent six months working with a local nutrition clinic, watching patients struggle with complex health apps. That experience convinced me: the best health tools are the simplest ones.

Today, BMI Tool serves thousands of users monthly across 40+ countries. We have been featured in several health blogs and continue to improve based on user feedback.
