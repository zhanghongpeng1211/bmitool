Why We Built This

I started paying attention to my weight after my uncle was diagnosed with type 2 diabetes. His doctor said his BMI had been elevated for a decade. "Just a number," he used to say. Turns out that number mattered.

When I went looking for a simple BMI calculator, everything was either buried in ads, required signup, or tried to sell me supplements. So I built BMI Tool — a clean, free calculator that does one thing well: tells you your BMI and what it means.

What We Believe

Health information should not be confusing. If you need a PhD to understand a calculator, it is probably not useful. We write like we are talking to a friend — honest, direct, no fluff. Every article is based on peer-reviewed research or clearly labeled personal experience.

We do not sell supplements. We do not push affiliate products as the main content. We do not collect your health data. This site exists because I wish it had existed when my uncle needed it.

Who This Is For

BMI Tool is for anyone curious about their weight status but overwhelmed by the noise online. Whether you are checking your BMI for the first time or tracking trends over months, you will find practical tools and real talk here.
