About BMI Tool

Evidence-based BMI analysis built by medical professionals for people who want real answers, not generic advice.

👨‍⚕️

Dr. David Chen

Board-Certified Internal Medicine Physician

I've spent 15 years in clinical practice, and one thing keeps bothering me: patients come in with BMI printouts from random websites that tell them they're "healthy" when they're clearly not, or "obese" when they're actually fine.

The problem isn't BMI itself — it's how it's explained. Most tools give you a number and a color. That's it. No context about age, ethnicity, muscle mass, or what the number actually means for your health.

I built BMI Tool because I was tired of seeing my patients confused by oversimplified calculators. This site uses the same clinical thresholds I use in my practice, with the nuance that actually matters.
